1. What is a Program?

A program is a set of instructions given to a computer to perform a specific task. We use different programs in our daily life, such as Instagram, Paytm, and Zomato. For example, when we use Paytm to send money, the app follows a program to complete the transaction safely and quickly.

4. Algorithm for Ordering Food on Swiggy

1. Open the Swiggy app.
2. Log in to your account.
3. Select a restaurant.
4. Choose the food items.
5. Add items to the cart.
6. Check the order details.
7. Select the delivery address.
8. Choose a payment method.
9. Make the payment.
10. Receive order confirmation.
